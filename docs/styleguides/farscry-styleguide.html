Material You Seed
Android seed color: #a78bfa. Material You processes this through HCT color space to generate tonal palettes for surfaces, on-colors, and containers. Users with dynamic theming may override this entirely with their wallpaper colors — this is intentional. The icon container remains static regardless of dynamic color.

iOS tint color: #a78bfa (dark mode), #7c3aed (light mode). Applied to navigation items, toggles, links, and system controls via the native tint API.

Voice & Tone

Farscry's copy follows the Loose Wire voice: direct, honest, zero fluff. But the product has its own angle — privacy without paranoia. It's not trying to scare you into caring about encryption. It just does the right thing and tells you plainly.

✓ Write like this
"Private video calls. Nothing else." "One Docker container. Your server, your data." "No phone number required. Share a friend code." "$3/month. We run the server. You just call." "Open source. AGPL-3.0."
✗ Not like this
"Military-grade encryption protects YOUR data" "Say goodbye to Big Tech surveillance!" "We're building the future of private communication" "Join our community of privacy advocates" "Powered by cutting-edge WebRTC technology"
Direct.

Say what it does. Skip the mission statement.

Calm.

Privacy without paranoia. No fear-mongering. No shouting.

Technical.

Docker, AGPL, WebRTC — name the real thing. The audience respects specifics.

Short.

Marketing copy should be half as long as you think. Then cut it again.

Native Apps

Farscry goes fully native on both platforms. This styleguide does not cover in-app UI — that's owned by iOS and Android. What this guide covers:

Element Owned by Notes
App icon This guide Ping on dark container. Identical on both platforms.
Seed / tint color This guide #a78bfa. Fed to Material You + iOS tint API.
Surfaces, backgrounds Platform Material You dynamic color / iOS system backgrounds.
Typography Platform SF Pro (iOS), Roboto (Android). No custom fonts in-app.
Navigation patterns Platform iOS: tab bar, swipe back. Android: Material nav, back gesture.
Components Platform Native buttons, toggles, inputs, sheets.
Dynamic color override User Android users may override seed with wallpaper colors. Allowed.
Why fully native? Trust. A privacy-focused calling app should feel like it belongs on your phone — not like a web view wearing a disguise. Native components also mean automatic support for accessibility features, text scaling, reduced motion, and platform-specific interaction patterns.
