Brand Guide · v1.0 · 2026

Loose Wire LLC is one person building focused software. Each product is independent, opinionated, and designed to solve one thing well. This brand is a maker's mark — not a corporate identity. It lives in footer credits, app store listings, and invoices. Products get the spotlight. This page is the entire brand guide.

04

Voice

Write like you'd talk to another dev at a meetup. Direct, honest, zero fluff. The brand has personality because it is a person.

Direct
Say what you built and who it's for. Skip the "we're on a mission to" preamble.
Honest
If something's an MVP, say so. Being the only person working on it is a feature, not a liability.
Casual, not sloppy
First person is fine. Contractions are fine. Exclamation points are rare. Emoji is absent.
Builder-first
Talk about what things do, not what they "empower." Technical details welcome. Buzzwords are not.
✓ Sounds like this
"I build niche tools for specific problems." "Solo dev, based in Utah." "Built with TanStack Start, runs on Cloudflare." "It records, transcribes, and gets out of the way."
✗ Never this
"Empowering the next generation of creators." "Our world-class team is passionate about..." "Leveraging cutting-edge AI to revolutionize..." "Join our incredible journey!"
